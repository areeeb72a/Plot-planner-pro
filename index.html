Plot Planner Pro User Manual

1. Setting Up Plot Dimensions

To start planning, configure the plot boundaries using the right sidebar panel (Irregular Plot Math Engine):

  • Enter dimensions for Side A (Top), Side B (Right), Side C (Bottom), and Side D (Left).
  • Enter Diagonal E (from top-left corner to bottom-right corner) to calculate irregular shapes using Heron's Formula. If empty, the average method will estimate the area with a warning.
  • Set your local Marla standard (e.g. Lahore Urban 225 sq ft or Rural 272.25 sq ft) to automatically calculate the area in Kanals and Marlas.
  • Configure the active units via the **Measurement Unit** selector, supporting Feet (ft), Meters (m), and **Yards (Karachi Yard - sq yds / yds)**.
  • If you are in Islamabad, click the preset buttons (8 Marla or 1 Kanal) to automatically populate standard boundary dimensions.

2. Canvas Drafting and Snap Controls

Draw walls and partition walls using the 2D drafting board:

  • Select Draw Line tool in the left toolbar.
  • Left-click on the canvas grid to start a line, move mouse to preview length, and click again to seal. Hold Shift while moving to force strictly horizontal or vertical walls.
  • The drafting board features smart snapping. Turn on Grid Snapping in the configuration settings to align wall corners automatically.

3. Regulatory Setbacks & Pin Dropping

  • Select your local municipal authority from the dropdown (LDA, CDA, RUDA, SBCA, PDA, or QDA) to display mandatory setbacks.
  • Standard statutory front/rear setbacks will draw as orange dashed guidelines on the canvas.
  • Select a pin tool (Water, Gas, or Electric) and click anywhere on the grid to tag utility connections on your plot.

4. Running Microclimate Simulations

Use the Geo-Energy & Microclimate panel to simulate weather patterns and solar paths:

  • Check Solar Path & Shadows to view the sun's trajectory. Slide the Time of Day slider to watch shadows cast from plot boundaries and neighbors rotate in real-time.
  • Check Animated Wind Vectors and toggle seasons to see hot summer breezes (SW) or cool winter drafts (NE) animated across the canvas.
  • Check Sound Pollution Gradient to see traffic noise decay zones inside the plot.

5. Estimating Construction Costs

  • Click BOM & Costs in the header actions. Review the estimated bricks, cement bags, sand, and tiles.
  • Edit the unit rates in the pricing table to match local market rates and view the updated grand total.
  • Switch roles in the header and leave team comments or municipality annotations in the approvals log.
  • Click Export Project to save a backup JSON database file of your entire project.
